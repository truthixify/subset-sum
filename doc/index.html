Subset Sum ZK Game

The Subset Sum game challenges players to select a subset of numbers from a given list to match a target sum. Utilizing zk-SNARKs for proof verification, the game ensures that player's solutions are correct without revealing the specific subset chosen, adding an extra layer of security and privacy. This combination of strategic thinking, problem-solving, and cryptographic technology offers an engaging way to explore mathematical concepts and logical reasoning.
